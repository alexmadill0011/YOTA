Learn to Train
1
Ages 8 & Under
Entry level developmental group. Focus on fun and safety in freestyle and backstroke with exposure to butterfly and breaststroke.
2
Ages 7–8
Building all four legal strokes with emphasis on kicking mechanics and technique-focused drills.
3
Ages 9–10
Developing aerobic foundation and conditioning with core-focused dryland and proprioception-heavy drill work. Proper technique is the expectation.
4
Ages 11–12
Introduction to competitive training: further developing aerobic foundation with stroke focus. Emphasis on goal setting, race strategy, and introduction to physical and mental wellness within sport.

1
Ages 13–15
Competitive senior developmental group. Focus on aerobic foundation, introduction to 200s of stroke, kick and drill emphasis, and beginning race strategy implementation in practice.
2
Ages 14 & Over
Continued focus on training for YMCA Nationals and competing at the national level. Progressing goal setting and learning to implement goal times in workouts under competitive duress.
3
Ages 14 & Over
Exceptional ability and proven national meet success. Training to develop elite race strategies and an advanced training regiment at the national level stage.
4
Ages 15 & Over (14 with coach discretion)
Elite training group focused on success at national level competition and beyond.
