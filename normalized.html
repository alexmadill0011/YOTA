Age Group Swimming

Aerobic Capacity

  • Increasing ability to swim age-appropriate distances.
  • Repeating distances on increasingly tight intervals with consistent results.

Example: 20 × 100 on 2:00 — aiming for 1:20–1:25 average by age 12.

Anaerobic Capacity

  • Increasing comfort with swimming fast for longer.
  • Repeating short fast efforts with consistent results on longer rest.

Example: 10 × 50 on 3:00 — aiming for +/−1 sec by age 12.
